How to Integrate Market Trend Data

Material Cost Tracking: Use platforms like S&P Global, Bloomberg, or Construction Material Indices to monitor material prices such as erosion blankets, hydroseeding materials, and geotextiles.

Labor Market Data: Track wage trends in various regions using data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), Indeed, or PayScale.

Equipment Cost and Fuel Prices: Monitor equipment rental rates and fuel costs using sources like RentalRateBlueBook, EquipmentWatch, or the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA).
