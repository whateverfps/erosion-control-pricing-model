Detailed Breakdown of Each Component

1. Labor Cost (L): Labor cost is calculated by multiplying the time required for each task by the hourly wage for each worker and the number of crew members.

2. Material Cost (M): Material cost is calculated by multiplying the quantity of materials by the price per unit.

3. Equipment Cost (E): Equipment cost includes the cost of renting equipment and associated transportation or fuel costs.

4. Regional and Environmental Adjustment (R_A): This adjustment accounts for geographic variations in labor and material costs.
