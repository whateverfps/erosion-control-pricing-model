Example Calculation

For a typical erosion control project, the breakdown is as follows:

  • Labor Cost (L): $10,000
  • Material Cost (M): $8,000
  • Equipment Cost (E): $5,000
  • Regional Adjustment (R_A): $460
  • Drive Time (D): $2,000
  • Permit and Compliance (P): $1,500
  • Inspection Cost (I): $1,000
  • Contingency Factor (C_f): $1,222
  • Total Project Cost (C): $29,358
